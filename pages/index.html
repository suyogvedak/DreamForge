About DreamForge

DreamForge is a TYBSc Computer Science project designed for writers, RPG designers, and worldbuilders. It blends procedural generation with AI to produce creative assets like characters, worlds, weapons, logos, and quests. Users can log in, save their creations, and export everything including 3D models.
